About me




I'm a French Back-End Software Engineer remotely working at Trusk and I occasionally do Freelance. I was born and raised in Paris but I recently moved in Novi Sad, Serbia.

I'm fond of Javascript/Node JS. I sometimes also use Python. In general, I prefer minimalist approaches rather than over-engineered solutions. In my work, I give a lot of importance to practices like functional programming, unit tests, documentation and open source.

When away from the computer, I enjoy traveling, hiking, martial arts, swimming, cooking, continuous learning and reading.
